Our Story

Catalyzing innovation by bringing together the world's brightest minds in technology, design, and business.

The Vision

Designing the Future, Together.

Since our inception in 2017, the Global Tech Summit was founded on a simple premise: the most profound technological leaps occur when diverse disciplines collide.

We don't just want to predict the future; we want to give you the blueprints to build it. By breaking down silos between artificial intelligence, cloud infrastructure, and human-centric design, we create an ecosystem where transformative ideas become reality.

Our Core Mission

"To democratize access to elite technological insights and foster collaborations that solve global challenges."

10 Years of Impact
Our Evolution

A Decade of Innovation

From a small meetup in a garage to a global phenomenon.

2017 - The Genesis

Started as a modest gathering of 200 developers in Austin, Texas. Focus was purely on emerging web technologies and open-source collaboration.

2020 - The Global Shift

Pivoted to a massive virtual summit reaching 15,000 attendees across 80 countries. Introduced the AI and Machine Learning track.

2024 - Return to Live

Moved headquarters to San Francisco. Integrated hardware, Web3, and sustainability tracks. Sold out our 4,000 capacity venue in just three days.

2027 - The 10th Edition

Our most ambitious event yet. Expecting 5,000+ attendees at the Moscone Center, with over 120 global tech luminaries shaping the conversation.

Our Commitment to the Future

We believe that a premium tech event must also lead by example in ethics, diversity, and environmental responsibility.

  • 🌍 Carbon Neutral Event

    We offset 100% of our carbon footprint, eliminate single-use plastics, and source catering locally.

  • 🤝 Inclusive Tech

    Our stage representation targets a 50/50 gender balance, and we offer a robust scholarship program for underrepresented groups.

  • 🛡️ Strict Code of Conduct

    We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment to ensure a safe, welcoming environment for all.
