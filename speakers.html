Speaker FAQs

When is the deadline to apply? +

The Call for Speakers for the 2027 Summit closes on July 1st, 2027. We review applications on a rolling basis, so we encourage early submissions.

What session formats are available? +

We offer three formats: 20-minute lightning talks, 45-minute deep-dive presentations, and 90-minute interactive masterclasses. You can select your preferred format in the application.

Do I need past speaking experience? +

Not necessarily! While experience helps for main stage keynotes, we have dedicated tracks for first-time speakers and offer mentorship programs to help you prepare your talk.
