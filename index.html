Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a discount for teams? +

Yes! We offer a 20% discount for groups of 3 or more attendees from the same company. The discount is automatically applied at checkout when you select the 'Team Pass' option.

Are the sessions recorded? +

All main stage keynotes and panel discussions are recorded. Pro and VIP pass holders will have lifetime access to the video library within 7 days after the event concludes.

Can I refund or transfer my ticket? +

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event. You can transfer your ticket to a colleague at any time up to 48 hours before the event starts through your attendee portal.
